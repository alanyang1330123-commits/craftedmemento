How We Charge

Transparent Revenue Model

Beyond subscriptions, our pricing is structured to align with your success

💰

Product Sales Margin

A percentage margin is added to each manufactured product ordered through the platform. This is our primary revenue source and scales with your order volume.

🚀

Premium Add-Ons

Optional enhancements such as expedited shipping, exclusive design elements, rush manufacturing, or priority customer support are available at additional per-use fees.

🤝

Licensing & Partnerships

Revenue from licensing agreements with artists, co-branded collections with tourism boards, and strategic partnerships with local businesses.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and platform

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No minimum order quantity on Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may have volume commitments discussed during onboarding.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What currencies do you accept?

All pricing is in CAD. We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and can arrange invoicing for Enterprise clients.

How does the product margin work?

When you order products through our platform, the wholesale manufacturing cost plus our platform margin is the price you pay. Volume discounts apply for large orders.

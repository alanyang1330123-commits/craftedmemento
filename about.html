Our Story

Revolutionizing the Souvenir Industry

CraftedMemento Creations is a pioneering online centralized souvenir design platform that caters specifically to tourism businesses and institutions with souvenir stores looking to create unique, personalized products.

Our platform seamlessly integrates design customization, centralized manufacturing, and global shipping — providing an all-in-one solution for businesses seeking bespoke and memorable merchandise.

We aim to be the "Vistaprint" or "Canva" in the centralized souvenir design and manufacturing field — empowering businesses worldwide to create souvenirs that truly represent their brand.

CraftedMemento is not just a service; it's a creative partner for tourism businesses globally, bringing personalized design, centralized manufacturing, and global shipping together in one innovative platform.

Design Customization

Intuitive online tools to add logos, design shapes representing unique landscapes or characters, and incorporate brand elements effortlessly.

State-of-the-art factory with cutting-edge molding technologies ensuring consistent quality, efficient timelines, and eco-friendly production.

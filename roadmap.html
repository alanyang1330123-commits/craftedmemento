Our Journey

Growth Roadmap

From launch in Victoria, BC to a globally recognized souvenir platform — our phased plan to transform the industry.

Milestones

Phase-by-Phase Expansion

A disciplined, data-driven path from local launch to global scale

March 2024

Phase 1 — Foundation

Company incorporation in Victoria, BC. Website launch. Initial supplier partnerships secured. Core team assembled and platform development begins.

Q3 2024

Phase 2 — Platform Launch

Online design tool goes live with 20+ templates. Centralized manufacturing hub operational. First batch of B2B clients onboarded from Victoria's local tourism sector.

Q1 2025

Phase 3 — AI Integration

AI-powered design recommendation engine deployed. Analytics dashboard launched for professional subscribers. Expanded product catalog to 12+ product categories.

Q3 2025

Phase 4 — Regional Growth

Expand across British Columbia and into Alberta. Partner with BC tourism boards for co-branded collections. Affiliate marketing program launched. 100+ active business clients.

2026 — 2027

Phase 5 — National Scale

Pan-Canadian market presence. Partnerships with national museum chains and university networks. Professional design services team scaled. 500+ active clients nationwide.

2028 — 2029

Phase 6 — Global Expansion

Entry into North American and European markets. Licensing partnerships with international tourism boards. Platform localized for multiple languages. 2,000+ global business clients.
